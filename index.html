About Me

In 2017, I made a leap from dedicated athleticism to the world of coding—a transition fueled by a passion for problem-solving. Challenges are my playground, and I approach each problem as an opportunity for ingenious solutions. The Covid-19 pivot redirected my path, leading me to wholeheartedly embrace the power of software development—a newfound passion reminiscent of my dedication to basketball.

My journey began with volunteer work at the local church, laying a solid foundation for diverse projects and freelancing endeavors. This trajectory eventually landed me a role as a Software Engineer. Driven, hungry for growth, and a fast learner, these attributes have been the driving force propelling my dynamic journey in this ever-evolving field.

My core lies in a robust set of technical skills, specializing in back-end development with languages like C++, PHP, Java, Python, and Scala. I navigate frameworks like Java Spring Boot, Spark Scala, PHP CodeIgniter, and Python Django/Flask with ease. On the front end, I'm well-versed in HTML5, CSS3, React, Angular, and Vue.js. Database management is a forte, covering MySQL, PostgreSQL, Microsoft SQL, CassandraDB, MongoDB, and Snowflake. Supported by expertise in ServiceNow, Grafana, and Azure Log Analytics, I've optimized processes, led data initiatives, and developed web applications—showcasing adaptability, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to quality throughout my professional journey.
